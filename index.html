Laundry Done Right

Simple tips to protect your clothes and get the best results every time.

👕

Sort Before You Wash

  • Separate lights, darks, and colors
  • Wash heavily soiled items separately
  • Keep delicates away from heavy fabrics
  • Check care labels before loading
🌀

Washing Best Practices

  • Use cold water for colors & delicates
  • Hot water for towels, bedding & whites
  • Don't overfill — clothes need room to move
  • Use the recommended detergent amount
♨️

Drying Tips

  • Low heat for synthetics & activewear
  • Medium heat for everyday cottons
  • High heat for towels and heavy linens
  • Remove clothes promptly to prevent wrinkles
  • Do not overfill — observe the maximum load line marked on the dryer door
Pro Tip: Always check garment care labels — symbols on the tag indicate the manufacturer's recommended wash and dry settings. When in doubt, go cooler and gentler to extend the life of your clothing.
